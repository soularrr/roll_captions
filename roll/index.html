FAQ

Questions, answered

Is ROLL free to use?

Yes. ROLL is free with no signup fees and no watermarks on your captions.

What platforms does it work for?

Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Threads, Facebook, and Pinterest — captions adjust in length and style to match each one.

Do I need to create an account?

You'll get a quick one-time sign-in prompt the first time you generate captions — this keeps the tool free for everyone. After that, you're set.

Can I upload a photo instead of describing my post?

Yes — drop a photo into the upload area and ROLL will base the captions on what's actually in the image.
